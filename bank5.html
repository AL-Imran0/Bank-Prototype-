HEPI — Human-centric Economic & Profitable Innovations
✦
What is HEPI?
HEPI — Human-centric Economic and Profitable Innovations — is a performance framework rooted in the higher objectives of Islam (Maqasid al-Islam). It measures whether your banking and financial activity is delivering real benefit to you, your community, and the environment — not just compliance with rules.
⊛
Why does it matter?
Traditional halal banking focuses on avoiding haram (prohibited). HEPI goes further: it asks what good is being achieved. A truly mature Islamic economy must reduce poverty, protect the environment, uphold dignity, and distribute wealth fairly — not just avoid interest.
The 5 HEPI Dimensions
👥
1. Human Value Creation
Living wages, skills development, worker dignity, consumer protection, community empowerment
📈
2. Economic Viability with Purpose
Shariah-compliant financing, equitable value distribution, fair supplier relations, long-term sustainability
⚖️
3. Ethical Alignment
Transparency, anti-corruption, zakat compliance, fair marketing, Shariah governance, contractual fairness
🌱
4. Environmental Stewardship
Green sukuk, carbon footprint, sustainable sourcing, waste reduction, eco-halal certification
🤝
5. Social Justice & Inclusion
Workforce diversity, vulnerable group access, community investment, grievance mechanisms, accessibility
🎯
