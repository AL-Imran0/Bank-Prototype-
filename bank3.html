✦ AI-Generated Priority Recommendations

HIGH PRIORITYInitiate Carbon Footprint Audit & Green Sukuk Framework
Environmental stewardship score (58/100) is the primary drag on HEPI performance. The maqasid framework requires environmental balance (hifz al-bi'ah). Engage a certified GHG auditor to assess Scope 1 & 2 emissions. Simultaneously develop a green sukuk framework aligned with AAOIFI Shariah Standard No. 62, targeting a pilot BDT 500M issuance by Q4 2025.
⏱ Timeline: Q2 2025 (audit) → Q4 2025 (sukuk pilot) · Estimated HEPI impact: +8 points
HIGH PRIORITYEstablish Formal Worker Voice & Grievance Mechanism (Shura Principle)
Worker consultation is currently limited to suggestion box (score: 2/5). Islamic principle of shura mandates meaningful participation. Establish a quarterly Staff Consultative Council with representation from all staff grades including contract workers. Implement a confidential whistleblower mechanism. Document all grievances, responses, and outcomes — this directly serves maqasid protection of human dignity (karamah).
⏱ Timeline: Q2 2025 · Estimated HEPI impact: +4 points
MEDIUM PRIORITYPublish Annual Zakat & Social Impact Report
Zakat is distributed (score: 3/5) but lacks public accountability. Publish a standalone Zakat & Social Impact Report verified by a recognized zakat authority. Include: total zakat calculated, nisab methodology, eligible recipient categories, amounts distributed, geographic reach. This transforms a private obligation into a public demonstration of maqasid commitment to wealth distribution (hifz al-mal).
⏱ Timeline: Q3 2025 · Estimated HEPI impact: +3 points
ONGOINGExpand Islamic Microfinance & Financial Inclusion Portfolio
Social justice score (74/100) is strong but microfinance can be scaled. Consider a dedicated Islamic Microfinance window using qard hasan and musharakah structures targeting women entrepreneurs in rural Bangladesh — mirroring Malaysia's successful model. Target 5,000 new microfinance accounts by 2026. Partner with NGOs for community outreach. Advances maqasid protection of lineage (nasl) and wealth distribution (mal).
⏱ Timeline: Q4 2025 – 2026 · Estimated HEPI impact: +5 points
