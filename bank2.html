HIGH PRIORITY Initiate Carbon Footprint Audit & Green Sukuk Framework
Your environmental stewardship score (58/100) is the primary drag on HEPI performance. The maqasid framework requires environmental balance (hifz al-bi'ah). Immediately engage a certified GHG auditor to assess Scope 1 & 2 emissions from bank operations. Simultaneously, develop a green sukuk framework aligned with AAOIFI Shariah Standard No. 62 for green instruments, targeting a pilot issuance of BDT 500M by Q4 2025 linked to renewable energy projects in Bangladesh.
⏱ Timeline: Q2 2025 (audit) → Q4 2025 (sukuk pilot) · Estimated HEPI impact: +8 points
HIGH PRIORITY Establish Formal Worker Voice & Grievance Mechanism
Current worker consultation limited to suggestion box (score: 2/5). Islamic principle of shura (consultation) mandates meaningful participation. Establish a quarterly Staff Consultative Council with representation from all grades including contract workers. Implement a confidential whistleblower mechanism with anonymity protection. Document all grievances, responses, and outcomes. This directly serves maqasid protection of human dignity (karamah).
⏱ Timeline: Q2 2025 · Estimated HEPI impact: +4 points
MEDIUM PRIORITY Publish Annual Zakat & Social Impact Report
Zakat is calculated and distributed (score: 3/5) but lacks public accountability. Publish a standalone Zakat & Social Impact Report verified by a recognized zakat authority (e.g., BAZS Bangladesh or equivalent). Include: total zakat calculated, nisab methodology, categories of eligible recipients, amounts distributed, geographic reach. This transforms a private obligation into a public demonstration of the bank's maqasid commitment to wealth distribution (hifz al-mal).
⏱ Timeline: Q3 2025 · Estimated HEPI impact: +3 points
ONGOING Expand Microfinance & Financial Inclusion Portfolio
Social justice score (74/100) is strong but microfinance can be scaled. Consider a dedicated Islamic Microfinance window using qard hasan and musharakah structures targeting women entrepreneurs in rural areas — this mirrors Malaysia's successful model. Set a target of 5,000 new microfinance accounts by 2026. Partner with NGOs for community outreach. This advances both maqasid protection of lineage (nasl) and wealth distribution (mal).
⏱ Timeline: Q4 2025 – 2026 · Estimated HEPI impact: +5 points
